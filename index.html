Welcome!

Hi, welcome to my personal travel blog. This is a place for community, experiences, excitent and sharing travel tips. My story is quite simple, I started travelling on my holidays as many of you, but soon came to realise that this was not enough. I made de desicion to quit my job and start a new career as a travel blogger. Starting was not easy, but with determination I created a new career and lifestyle that fulfills me. Get to know me

Who I am

I was born in Barcelona, but I moved to Dublin in 2018. In Dublin is where I discover my love for travelling and I started building up my new career path.

What do I do?

For the past two years, I have been travelling for a living and sharing my experiences with whoever wants to listen. It has been an amazing oportunity meeting so many people from different places, and for that I am truly garteful

Solo Female Travel

Solo Female Travel has been the cornestone of my project. Since I started blogging main focus was to create a safe space for women to share experiences, tips, and overall create friendships.

Why solo female travel?

As women, we need to be extra careful with everything we do, especially when travelling. When I went on my first trip I remember being very scared, but why should we? Exactly, we should not. This is how my idea came about. Since then I have been building this community so we can help each other while enjoy this wonderful world.
